Frequently Asked Questions

What is DevFund?



DevFund offers technology-based investments to startups whose technology is the
business in exchange for a share/equity. This helps innovators and founders of tech startups
in building their technology solutions at minimal or even zero expense.

In addition to the given solution, this investment encompasses the provision of an Interim
CTO (Chief Technology Officer) who provides essential technical direction for the startup.

What are the direct benefits of DevFund?



DevFund helps your startup by:

-Securing the technical partnership needed to drive your business’s success.

-Achieving market dominance by guiding your product evolution for optimal fit and impact.

-Providing an interim CTO on-demand for as long as you need one.
